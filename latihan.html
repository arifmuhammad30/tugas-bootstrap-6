Montevideo City

Montevideo is the capital, main port and largest city of Uruguay (population 1.8 million out of a total of 3.43 million in Uruguay as of 2004).

Punta Del Este

Punta del Este is a town and resort located on the Atlantic Ocean coast of Maldonado Department, southeastern Uruguay. Come enjoy its beauty

Colonia Del Sacramento

Colonia del Sacramento is a city in southwestern Uruguay, near the Río de la Plata, facing Buenos Aires, Argentina. It is one of the oldest cities in Uruguay and the capital of the Colonia Department.

Cabo Polonio

Cabo Polonio is a hamlet located on the east coast of Uruguay in the Rocha Department. Cabo Polonio has no roads leading to it and is located about 7 km from the main highway.

Punta Del Diablo

Punta del Diablo is a village and seaside region in Uruguay, Rocha Department, 298 kilometers east of the capital Montevideo.
